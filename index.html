Welcome to the GetComputersEverywhere webstite

The GetComputersEverywhere project is a project to help children who do not have access to computers or the internet. This website is designed to advertise designs of wireless routers and to collect computers that people do not use anymore, so children who do not have access to technology will be able to use technology and learn how to use the internet.

We hope you will enjoy browsing the GetComputersEverywhere site

The fixnonet

The fixnonet is a router that operates off the cellular network. It should run off batteries because the conditions that it might be in may not have a mains power supply. It is designed to help people who have a laptop but no internet access. It will be powered by a sim card module from Adafruit.
